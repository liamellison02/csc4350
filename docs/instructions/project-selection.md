Please discuss your project choice for the semester.
Each project is required to use a database in its design.
You will create a presentation to select and describe your semester long project.
Password generators and To Do list apps are not allowed.

Your presentation will have a maximum of 7 slides (10 minutes) that discuss the following:
- What problem(s) does your software hope to solve?
- What is one competing product?
- How will your software be unique from its competitors?
- Who are the people your app hopes to help?
- How will the feature set help them?
- Can your app be generated simply by prompting an LLM/agent?
  - If so, what features should be added or what more difficult problem can you solve?

Only one member per team will have to submit your slides.
All members will have to participate in the presentation of your project selection.

Teams will present in Team Order (Team 1 will be first, followed by Team 2....).
